# Minecraft Plugins

Welcome to the plugin documentation hub. Explore powerful, feature-rich plugins designed to enhance your Minecraft server experience.

## Available Plugins

### BlissGems
**A comprehensive gem system with unique abilities and energy mechanics**

BlissGems introduces 8 unique gems to your server, each with special abilities that players can unlock and upgrade. Features an advanced energy system with gem states ranging from BROKEN to PRISTINE+5, providing endless progression opportunities.

**Key Features:**
- 8 unique gem types with distinct abilities
- Energy system with 11 progressive states
- Seamless Oraxen integration
- Customizable abilities and cooldowns
- Gem degradation and repair mechanics

[View Documentation →](/plugins/blissgems/)

---

### BetterStasis
**Enhanced stasis mechanics for Pearl Canon gameplay**

BetterStasis improves the vanilla ender pearl stasis experience, making Pearl Canon gameplay more reliable and user-friendly. Perfect for technical servers and creative transportation systems.

**Key Features:**
- Improved stasis chamber mechanics
- Reliable pearl tracking system
- Configurable timeout settings
- Player-friendly notifications
- Lightweight and performant

[View Documentation →](/plugins/betterstasis/)

---

### GeminiMC
**AI-powered Minecraft assistant with autonomous task execution**

GeminiMC brings advanced AI capabilities to your Minecraft server through Google's Gemini API. Execute commands, automate tasks, and interact with your server using natural language.

**Key Features:**
- Natural language command execution
- Autonomous task system
- Context-aware AI responses
- Configurable safety settings
- Multi-player support

[View Documentation →](/plugins/geminimc/)

## General Requirements

All plugins are designed for modern Minecraft servers and share these common requirements:

- **Minecraft Version:** 1.21+ (check individual plugin docs for specifics)
- **Server Software:** Paper, Purpur, or compatible forks
- **Java Version:** Java 17 or higher
